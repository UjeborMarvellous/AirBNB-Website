About Suite Rosso

Cozzy . Welcoming

Historical Charm

Nestled in the heart of Jesi, Suite Rosso 24 combines timeless charm with cultural richness. Explore the city's historic center, encircled by medieval walls, and visit iconic landmarks like Pergolesi Theatre and Palazzo della Signoria. Beyond Jesi, uncover the beauty of the Marche hills, savor world-famous Verdicchio wine, and visit enchanting villages like Cupramontana and Cingoli, the "Balcony of Le Marche." Experience elegance, history, and unforgettable hospitality.
