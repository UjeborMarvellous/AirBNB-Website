Suite Rosso 24 Jesi is a charming and cozy apartment in Jesi, ideal for couples, families, or solo travelers. Discover Jesi

Jesi is a city steeped in history and culture, known as the birthplace of Emperor Frederick II and home to exceptional wine production, including the famous Verdicchio. Whether you're strolling through its cobblestone streets, visiting its theaters, or savoring the local cuisine, Jesi offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity that will make your stay unforgettable.
